Victor is a unique chess set that includes cubical figures designed to explain movements of each figure.
It comes in innovative 4-piece chessboard with discretely installed magnets that keeps it closed and easy to open.
When not in use the chess box can be placed vertically, becoming chess tower with small footprint while becoming minimalist interior detail.
Each figure has dents on each side to explain their directions, except the king and the queen because of their significant role in the game.
Design, modeling, digital renderings and post-production by Darko Nikolić
