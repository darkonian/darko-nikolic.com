UO play is 3-axis stabilizing hand-held gimbal designed for smartphones and action cameras.
Designed with reliability and durability in mind, it is made out of machined weapon-grade aluminum to be rigid and premium-built at the same time. It uses rechargeable and swapable standard batteries for added convenience.
Each figure has dents on each side to explain their directions, except the king and the queen because of their significant role in the game.
Design, modeling, digital renderings and post-production by Darko Nikolić
