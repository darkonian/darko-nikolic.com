Y was a quest to find a form that keeps the time still.

It is found in object that speaks about the beauty of the moment...

Y wine glass is designed to be exclusive piece of stemware made with special handcrafting technique by skilled craftsmen.

During the manufacturing process while glass is enough hot for shaping air is injected through small metal tube to form bubble inside the base.

Design, modeling, digital renderings and post-production by Darko Nikolić
