Darko Nikolić is an industrial designer focused on innovation and technology with over 10 years of professional experience gained in Europe and China by working for international clients.

Extensive educational and practical knowledge from different creative fields and passion for technology brought him to China where he was leading design development for leading drone producers like AI Bird and Ehang.

Often very simple yet highly innovative and sophisticated, his designs are answers to complex technical, production, business and marketing requirements with a major focus on user experience and exceptional attention to the smallest detail.

Through years, Darko was working for numerous international clients, he is flexible into implementing different approaches and skill sets in different corporate or consultancy environments by emphasizing client's identity and vision.

His works are featured in online and printed media worldwide.

Darko is currently based in Shenzhen, available for freelance, contract or full-time opportunities.
