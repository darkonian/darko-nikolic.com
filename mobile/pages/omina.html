Omina is a stacking chair concept made of bent metal frame coated with two peaces of textile for body support.
It is inspired by Thonet's wooden chairs and designed in retro-modern style to be easy and fast produced on large industrial scale.
The whole construction gives certain elasticity to suit different users by maintaining enough structural stability for comfortable sitting.
The chair could be used indoor and outdoor and it is is suitable for commercial or residential use.
