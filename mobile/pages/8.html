8 skateboard tool is designed to be the smallest and most ergonomic skateboard adjustment tool. Because of its shape, small size and weight,
it allows to be stored in average stash pocket while skateboarding and to easy and fast adjust skateboard when necessary.
The double inbus key allows easier replacement of the board than with any other tool available on market and keeps the hardware perfectly aligned.
It has also two phillips extensions that are stored inside for different types of standardized skateboard hardware.

Prototypes displayed here are made from carbon fiber. Aluminum version is suitable for industrial production.
