Victor is unique chess set that includes cubical figures made out of styrofoam coated with plastic.
It comes in innovative 4-piece chessboard with discretely installed neodymium magnets that keeps it closed and very easy to open.
When closed it could be placed vertically to save space on shelf and also become interesting detail especially in minimalist interiors.

Each figure has dents on each side to explain their directions, except the king and the queen because of their significant role in the game.

Design, modeling, digital renderings and post-production by Darko Nikolić
