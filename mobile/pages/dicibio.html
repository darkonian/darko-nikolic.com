Dicibio is a unique and simple armchair that can be made of various materials by using special production technique.
Made of plastic, plywood, metal or advanced composite materials it creates structural support that is elastic and adapts to user's sitting position and weight. With the impact of user's weight, the armrests are spreading sideways by changing the angle of the backrest. The seat is separable and shaped as an inverted cone, enabling easy and simple cleaning when needed. Its simple form allows many possibilities for customization with different combinations of colors, patterns or stickers.
Depending on materials used the design can be suitable for indoor, outdoor, residential or commercial use.

With side turn it could switch its colors or patterns and be turned in opposite direction for sitting
