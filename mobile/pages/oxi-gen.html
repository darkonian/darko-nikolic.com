Oxi-gen is futuristic furniture collection suitable for CNC production from single panel materials with great efficiency, transport in flat packages, to be easily assembled or disassembled without tools, durable in use, easily, fully and infinitely recyclable. It requires extremely low producton tolerances so that parts can stuck with small tensions firmly after the assemblage.
It is designed for current trend of working migrations where relocation is often followed by problems with transport and life in small apartments.
Inspired by oxygen here is displayed in transparent plexiglass that contributes to overall aesthetics of the design.
