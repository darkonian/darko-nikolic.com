O bottle opener is a bottle opener whose circular shape allows to open a bottle without looking at it.
Its shape and size use small amount of metal which make it very durable, ergonomic, reliable, easy to produce and also fully recyclable. After opening a bottle it can be used as a coaster..

Its low production cost allows it to be produced in enormous series and shared for free by beverage producers during their promotions.
