Curve is unique furniture set with construction based on curved rails coated with synthetic foam. It allows great possibilities for customization and padding with different types of textile or synthetic leather. It is designed to be soft for sitting and safe for public use because there are no sharp edges or solid surfaces that might spontaneously hurt someone.
Digital renderings by Nabojša Glavonić. Design, modeling and post-production by Darko Nikolić.
