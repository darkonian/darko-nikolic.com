O bottle opener is a bottle opener whose circular shape allows users to open a bottle without looking at it. Made of a singe piece of metal, it is very durable, ergonomic, reliable, easy to produce and fully recyclable.
After opening a bottle it can be used as a coaster.
Its design is not suitable for small hands which keeps children safe from consuming beverage drinks.
